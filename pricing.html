Drop-In Tutoring

Do you need math help right away? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered with our Drop-In Tutoring service at [Your Tutoring Center Name]! With Drop-In Tutoring, you don’t have to book an appointment in advance. Just come to our center during our Drop-In hours and our tutors will be ready to assist you. Whether you have a simple math problem or a complex one, our tutors will provide you with personalized and effective support. And the best part is, you only pay for the time you use, with a rate of $1 per minute for the first 30 minutes, and then $0.75 per minute thereafter. Or, if you want unlimited access to our tutors during Drop-In hours, you can sign up for our Unlimited Drop-In Tutoring Plan for only $99 per month. Experience the convenience and flexibility of our Drop-In Tutoring and achieve your math goals with ease.

Here's how our Drop-In Tutoring works:

  • You can visit our center during operating hours without an appointment.
  • Upon arrival, check-in at the front desk and let our staff know that you would like to join a Drop-In Tutoring session.
  • Our staff will assign you to an available tutor who can assist you with your math questions and concerns.
  • Please note that Drop-In Tutoring sessions are limited to a certain duration, typically 30 minutes, to ensure that all students can receive assistance in a timely manner.
  • Drop-In Tutoring is offered on a first-come, first-served basis, and availability may vary depending on the number of students seeking assistance.
  • While Drop-In Tutoring is a convenient option, we recommend scheduling regular tutoring sessions to receive consistent support and maximize your learning progress.

Refund Policy

We understand that circumstances may change, and you may need to request a refund for unused tutoring sessions. Our refund policy is as follows:

  • If you request a refund before the start of your first scheduled session, you will be eligible for a full refund of the amount paid.
  • If you request a refund after the start of your first scheduled session but before completing 50% of the total purchased sessions, you will be eligible for a pro-rated refund minus any applicable fees.
  • No refunds will be provided for sessions that have been completed or for cancellation requests received after completing 50% of the total purchased sessions.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise, and you may need to cancel a scheduled tutoring session. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • If you need to cancel a session, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be subject to a cancellation fee of $10 per session.
  • Please note that the cancellation fee will be deducted from any applicable refund.
  • Repeated cancellations or failure to provide sufficient notice may result in the loss of future session slots or additional charges.

Makeup Policy

If you are unable to attend a scheduled tutoring session, we offer a makeup session option, subject to tutor availability. Our makeup policy is as follows:

  • If you provide at least 24 hours' notice of your inability to attend a session, we will make every effort to reschedule the session within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Makeup sessions are subject to tutor availability and must be scheduled within a specified period, typically within 14 days.
  • If a makeup session cannot be scheduled due to tutor availability or other constraints, an alternative arrangement, such as extending the duration of future sessions, may be considered.

Registration Fee

A one-time registration fee of $25 is required for all new students. This fee helps cover administrative costs, assessment materials, and account setup.
